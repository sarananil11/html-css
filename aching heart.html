SARAN ANIL

Aching Heart

It was all a dream, I guess

It suddenly felt through me

That voice of hers, sweet vocal cords

Made my sleep disturbed

It was a sunny day

When I fell in love with her

Suddenly rain deluged from the sky

Never mind, it was my tears

She wore her makeup as usual

And I wore my "mask" as usual

She kissed my cheeks as if it was my funeral

Guess what? I couldn't kiss her back

She went out to pursue her dreams

And here I am, without dreams anymore

All my fears have become real

Yet she couldn't give a damn about that

I opened my computer to start coding

She left a message that she'd be going

To one of her "friend's" birthday parties

Here I am sitting in the room with no company

I checked my messages and emails while

I drank a cup of coffee

I thought she'd bought sugar yesterday

Never mind, why blame her for my poor cup of coffee?

The sky is the limit, they say

I guess there is no limit for heartache

The heart is only safe when it is

And is not when it actually is

I don't even know what I'm thinking

Did I mention my girlfriend? I guess I did

The love of my life, the soul of my heaven

Drank blood from my heart like a raven

I opened my phone which she used last night

Sandy and she talked a little, I surmised

She doesn't even hide it anymore

I mean, why anymore? After all of this

She came at six when I was sitting on my chair

Rotten like an old tomato that stank

She screamed at me, God knows why

But I get it; after all, I'm her boyfriend on paper, aren't I?

I know she knows and she knows I know

It was just a matter of time for everything

I confronted her, asking about her relationships

And she tried to love me more than she ever did

Women love their men when they get caught

Is it a universal truth or is it just her?

Didn't have time to think when she stabbed

Into my heart where the love for her belongs

It was a 7-inch knife with a clean surface

I speculate she was waiting for this "ask me out"

I fell to the ground with a loud voice

Which was all in my head

I woke up fogged with dread

She asked what happened in my nightmare

I kissed her and made myself a coffee

And I saw a beautiful 7-inch clean-surfaced object

Tears ran down and my heart started pounding

I couldn't remember anything else

My heart ached like a monkey shot into space

It ached, ached and ached...
